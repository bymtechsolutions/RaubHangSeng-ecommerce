# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Malaysian families, food buyers, and restaurant purchasers ordering premium Pahang river fish for home cooking or wholesale use. They need fast confidence in freshness, processing quality, delivery coverage, and how to place an order.

## Product Purpose

Raub Hang Seng Fisheries is an ecommerce landing and ordering experience for fresh-frozen river fish. The site should communicate local source credibility, cold-chain reliability, and make WhatsApp or cart checkout feel direct and trustworthy.

## Brand Personality

Fresh, grounded, precise, and local. The page should feel like clean river water and a serious fishery operation, not a generic SaaS template or loud marketplace.

## Anti-references

Avoid full white glare backgrounds, generic purple-blue gradients, card-heavy AI landing-page scaffolds, decorative glassmorphism, beige/cream default palettes, and fake-looking stock atmospheres.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with real fishery imagery and source credibility.
2. Keep surfaces calm and easy on the eyes with river-tinted neutrals.
3. Reserve strong blue and green for navigation, purchase actions, and delivery trust.
4. Make every order path obvious: shop, cart, WhatsApp, delivery coverage.
5. Keep Chinese-first content readable, dense enough for commerce, and not overly decorative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Maintain visible focus states, comfortable reading backgrounds, reduced-motion-safe animation, and touch targets suitable for mobile buyers.
